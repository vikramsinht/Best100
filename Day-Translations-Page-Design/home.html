Human Translation Services: The Key to Accuracy

What makes it a human translation service?

Translation is the conversion of information from one language to another. Human translation is the communication of a message from a source language to an end language, performed by a trained professional in both dialects; giving it that human touch.

Translation services provide an opportunity to get your point across to an international audience. And with human translation services, you can uplevel your global reach in ways your business has never experienced before.

But how?

Human translators read more than just the words on the page. Human translators understand intention, tone of voice, and need. We also believe that human powered translations are the only way to maintain that heart in each translation.

Language Service Provider – How We Work

Localization is only possible with a human translator. The humans powering a localized translation look deeper than the actual words on the page. They look at what those words will mean to the culture receiving them.

This prevents publishing words that are perfectly normal to one culture, but extremely offensive to another. It saves time with phrases like, “De tal palo, tal astilla," which directly translates to, “Of such a stick, that splinter.” To most cultures, these are just words thrown together, but to Spanish speaking cultures, this phrase is completely relevant.
